Getting help




The preferred place to put a question about Yadex is the yadex mailing list. Alternatively, you can mail the maintainer (me). I'll be happy to help you if I can. Just be nice with me by not making me answer the same questions over and over. Many common questions are already answered in the documentation.

General Doom editing questions are better answered on rec.games.computer.doom.editing or in one of the many fine Doom editing tutorials and books (see the list).
